About Us

DRAAMILA EXIM INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD is an Import and Export Company based in Kerala (India) engaged in import and export of Grocery’s, fresh fruits, Vegetables, spices, Handicrafts, Garments, Coir Products and Coconuts. It always comes down to how customers endure the brand and how our brand makes our customers feel, we ensure your satisfaction through flawless quality. We provide high-end quality products at a proven and reasonable price placing ourselves between the “too cheap” and “too expensive” scenario.

Our Team

With the help of our qualified and dedicated team of professionals, we are able to reach the heights of success. Our professionals have a thorough knowledge of every aspect related to products. Further, they also keep a check of the contracted farmers and associated vendors and ensure that our products are free from all impurities. With their honest and hard work, we have been able to promote wellness through products across the globe.
